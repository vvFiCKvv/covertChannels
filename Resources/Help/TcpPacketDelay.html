TCP Packet Delay Help


A network covert channel is a mechanism that can be used to leak information across a network in violation of a security policy and in a manner that can be dificult to detect. Specific in the Tcp Packet Delay Covert Channel we can hide information on the distance time between two continues packets. For example if the time between two continues packets is less than 100ms we assume that transmited the bit zero, in addition if the above time is more than 100 we assume that transmeted the bit one.
